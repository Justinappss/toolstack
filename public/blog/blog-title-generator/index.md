## Why Blog Titles Matter for SEO

Click-through rate (CTR) is a ranking signal. A title that earns a 12% CTR vs a 4% CTR on the same position pushes your ranking up over time. The three elements that move CTR most: a number, a year, and a clear benefit or outcome.
