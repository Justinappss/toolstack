## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What is RankSpot?**

RankSpot is an AI SEO tool that automatically researches keywords, writes SEO-optimised articles, and publishes them to your blog on a daily schedule. It combines keyword research, competitor gap analysis, and AI content generation into a single autopilot workflow — designed for bloggers and site owners who want consistent content output without the manual work.

---

**Q: Does RankSpot actually rank content in Google?**

Results vary significantly by niche and competition level. In low-to-medium competition niches, RankSpot-generated content can achieve page-two and page-one rankings within 4–8 weeks of publication, particularly for long-tail queries. In highly competitive niches, AI-only content without human editing and link building typically underperforms hand-crafted content. RankSpot is most effective as a first-draft and publication engine, not a complete replacement for editorial judgment.

---

**Q: How does RankSpot's keyword research work?**

RankSpot identifies low-competition keyword gaps by comparing your site's existing content against competitors using GSC data integration. It then prioritises keywords with ranking potential based on your site's current domain authority and existing topical clusters. The output is a prioritised content queue that publishes automatically.

---

**Q: What is RankSpot's pricing?**

RankSpot operates on a subscription model with tiered plans based on the number of articles published per month. Pricing starts from approximately $49/month for a basic plan. A free trial or demo is available — check rankspot.io for current pricing, as plans are updated regularly.
