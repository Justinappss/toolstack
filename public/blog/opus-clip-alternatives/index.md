# Is Opus Clip Still King? We Tested 3 New AI Rivals (2026)

We ran the same 45-minute podcast through Opus Clip, Vidyo.ai, Captions.ai, and Submagic. Opus Clip produced the most usable clips (22, 85% keeper rate) and is the only tool with virality scoring and B-Roll AI. But Captions.ai beats it on transcript accuracy, and Submagic wins on hook rewriting.

## Executive Summary

1. **Opus Clip still leads overall:** 22 clips, 85% keeper rate, and the only tool with virality scoring + B-Roll AI + social scheduler in one package.
2. **Captions.ai wins on accuracy:** 98% caption accuracy — the highest of any tool tested. Worth it if you have accessibility or accuracy-first requirements.
3. **Submagic wins on hooks:** The strongest AI hook rewriting of the four tools, starting at $20/month — the cheapest paid option tested.
4. **Vidyo.ai is the closest rival:** Fastest processing at 11 minutes, social scheduler included, but starts at $29/month — more expensive than Opus Clip's Starter with fewer features.

## What We Tested

Controlled test: the exact same 45-minute solo podcast episode, processed through four tools on the same day, using each tool's default settings. Four metrics tracked: clips generated, processing time, keeper rate, and caption accuracy.

**Test conditions:**
- Source video: 45-minute solo podcast, clean audio, single speaker
- Settings: Default on all tools — no custom prompts or manual overrides
- Plan used: Entry-level paid plan on each tool
- Keeper rate: Clips we would publish without any further editing
- Caption accuracy: Errors per 100 words, converted to percentage

## The Contenders

### Opus Clip — THE BENCHMARK
**Rating: 8.4/10 | From $19/mo | Free: 60 min/mo**

The market leader in AI video repurposing since 2022. Opus Clip's ClipAnything model identifies the most engaging moments, exports 9:16 vertical clips with captions and virality scores. With 10M+ users and $68M raised.

**Strengths:** Virality scoring (0–100 per clip), B-Roll AI on Pro, Social scheduler (5 platforms), Best keeper rate in our test: 85%
**Weakness:** Credit model (per input minute) surprises new users

### Vidyo.ai — CLOSEST RIVAL
**Rating: 7.1/10 | From $29/mo | Free: 75 min/mo**

Similar approach to Opus Clip but differentiates with the most generous free tier (75 min/month) and faster processing. Lacks B-Roll AI and virality scoring.

**Strengths:** Most generous free tier: 75 min/mo, Fastest processing: 11 min, Social scheduler included, Auto reframe on all plans
**Weakness:** No virality scoring, no B-Roll AI, more expensive than Opus Clip at entry

### Captions.ai — CAPTION CHAMPION
**Rating: 7.4/10 | From $29/mo | Free: 50 min/mo**

Started as caption-first mobile app, expanded into full clip generation. Standout feature: 98% caption accuracy. Lower clip volume (12 clips from 45-min test vs 22 for Opus Clip).

**Strengths:** Highest caption accuracy: 98%, Clean fast interface, Auto reframe included, Fastest processing: 9 min
**Weakness:** Fewest clips generated, no virality scoring, no social scheduler

### Submagic — HOOK SPECIALIST
**Rating: 6.8/10 | From $20/mo | Free: 10 videos/mo**

Most affordable paid tool. Positions as caption and hook tool rather than full repurposing workflow. AI hook rewriting — rewrites opening lines for scroll-stopping impact — is the strongest of the four tools.

**Strengths:** Best AI hook rewriting, Cheapest paid plan: $20/mo, High caption quality: 92%, Simple workflow
**Weakness:** Lowest keeper rate (61%), no auto reframe, no B-Roll AI

## Who Should Switch

**Consider switching to Captions.ai** if you produce medical, legal, or educational content where caption errors matter.

**Consider switching to Submagic** if you already have clips and want an affordable hook-rewriting layer at $20/mo.

**Consider switching to Vidyo.ai** if you need the most generous free tier (75 min/mo) before committing to paid.

**Don't switch if:**
- You need virality scoring — only Opus Clip and Vidyo.ai offer it, and Opus Clip's is more mature
- You want B-Roll AI — no alternative tool includes it at any tier
- You're publishing across 5 platforms — Captions.ai has no social scheduler
- You want best cost-per-publishable-clip — Opus Clip wins at $0.33/clip

**The stack approach:** Opus Clip (clip detection + virality scoring) → Submagic (hook rewriting on top 5 clips) → native platform upload. Combined cost ~$39–49/mo.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What are the best Opus Clip alternatives in 2026?**
The three strongest are Vidyo.ai, Captions.ai, and Submagic. Vidyo.ai is the closest overall rival. Captions.ai is best for caption accuracy (98%). Submagic stands out for hook rewriting. None fully replaces Opus Clip's end-to-end workflow.

**Q: Is Vidyo.ai better than Opus Clip?**
Not overall. Vidyo.ai is faster (11 min vs 14 min) but generates fewer usable clips (72% keeper rate vs 85%), lacks virality scoring and B-Roll AI, and costs more at entry ($29/mo vs $19/mo).

**Q: Is Captions.ai worth it for captions only?**
Yes, if accurate captions are your core requirement. It achieved 98% accuracy in our test. However it only generated 12 clips vs 22 for Opus Clip, lacks virality scoring and social scheduling.

**Q: What is Submagic best for?**
Hook rewriting. Its AI rewrites the opening line of each clip for stronger scroll-stopping impact — the best of the four tools tested. Best used as a finishing layer on top of clips from another tool.

**Q: Which tool has the best free plan?**
Vidyo.ai by raw minutes (75/mo). But Opus Clip's free plan is most useful because it includes full feature access (virality scoring, auto reframe, auto captions) with a watermark.

**Q: Does Opus Clip still have the best virality scoring?**
Yes. Only Opus Clip and Vidyo.ai include virality scoring. Opus Clip's implementation is more mature — a 0–100 score per clip vs Vidyo.ai's quality rating.

**Q: What's the cheapest way to repurpose video in 2026?**
Submagic at $20/mo, followed by Opus Clip Starter at $19/mo. For a complete workflow with virality scoring, Opus Clip Starter delivers the most capability per dollar.
