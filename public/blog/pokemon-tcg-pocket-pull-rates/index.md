## Summary

Pokémon TCG Pocket publishes pull rates officially in-game under Pack Points. Per 5-card pack: ex 5.000%, Art Rare 2.572%, Super Rare 0.500%, Immersive Rare 0.222%, God Pack 0.050%, Crown Rare 0.040%.

Packs needed for 50% probability: ex 14, Art Rare 27, Super Rare 139, Immersive 312, God Pack 1,386, Crown Rare 1,733.

At 2 free packs per day: ex takes 7 days, Crown Rare takes 867 days.

The pity system guarantees ◆◆◆◆ every 10 packs from the same set — does not affect rarer pulls.
