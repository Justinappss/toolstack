## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What is Genspark for Word?**

Genspark for Word is a free Microsoft Word add-in that brings AI drafting, live web research, and image generation directly into your Word document. It's available from the Microsoft AppSource and requires no paid subscription — unlike Microsoft Copilot, which requires a Microsoft 365 Copilot licence at $30/user/month.

---

**Q: Is Genspark for Word actually free?**

Yes — the core features (AI drafting, research, image generation) are available at no cost. Genspark operates a freemium model with a daily credit system. For most casual and moderate users, the free tier is sufficient. Heavy daily use may require a paid plan.

---

**Q: How does Genspark compare to Microsoft Copilot?**

Copilot integrates more deeply with your existing Microsoft 365 data (emails, Teams, SharePoint) and is the better choice if you're already on a Microsoft 365 Business or Enterprise plan. Genspark wins on accessibility — it's free, requires no enterprise licence, and its live web research feature pulls current information into your document in real time, which Copilot doesn't do natively without Bing integration.

---

**Q: Can Genspark generate images inside Word?**

Yes — Genspark's image generation feature creates AI images directly within the add-in panel, which you can insert into your document. The quality is solid for blog headers, presentation visuals, and report illustrations. It uses a diffusion-based model and produces images in multiple aspect ratios.
