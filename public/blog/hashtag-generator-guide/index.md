## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What is the 3-tier hashtag strategy?**

The 3-tier strategy mixes hashtags by competition level: Tier 1 — broad high-volume tags (1M+ posts) for discovery exposure, Tier 2 — mid-size niche tags (50K–500K posts) where you can realistically rank, and Tier 3 — micro-niche tags (under 50K posts) where engagement rates are highest. A balanced mix across all three tiers consistently outperforms using only one type.

---

**Q: How many hashtags should I use on Instagram in 2026?**

Instagram's own guidance (as of 2024) recommends 3–5 highly relevant hashtags rather than the previous advice of using 30. Algorithms now penalise hashtag stuffing and reward relevance. For TikTok, 3–5 is also standard. For LinkedIn, 3 hashtags is the sweet spot — LinkedIn's algorithm actively deprioritises posts with more than 5 hashtags.

---

**Q: What are the biggest hashtag mistakes?**

Using only high-competition tags (you'll never rank against accounts with millions of followers), copy-pasting the same set every post (Instagram's algorithm detects repetitive patterns and reduces your reach), and using irrelevant hashtags to chase volume (which attracts the wrong audience and tanks your engagement rate).
