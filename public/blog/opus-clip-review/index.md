## What Is Opus Clip?

Opus Clip (opus.pro) is an AI video repurposing platform founded in 2022 by Young Zhao and Grace Wang. The product converts long-form videos — podcasts, webinars, YouTube content — into short-form clips optimized for TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts.

Key facts: 10M+ users, $68M raised (SoftBank Vision Fund 2), $215M valuation, 133 employees, $10.3M ARR.

## How It Works

1. Upload or paste a YouTube/Google Drive URL
2. AI analyzes for hooks, emotional peaks, and quotable moments — scores each clip 0–100 for virality potential
3. Download watermarked clips (free) or clean exports (paid)

**Credit model:** 1 credit = 1 minute of input video. A 60-minute podcast costs 60 credits.

## Core Features

- **AI Virality Score** — ranks clips 0–100 based on hook strength, pacing, and topic relevance
- **Auto Captions** — 95% accuracy, animated word-by-word, 48 languages
- **ClipAnything** — search transcripts by topic or keyword, jump to that moment
- **Auto Reframe** — 16:9 → 9:16 with face/action tracking
- **AI Hook Generator** — rewrites your opening 3 seconds for stronger retention
- **Social Scheduler** — publish to TikTok, Instagram, LinkedIn, YouTube Shorts from one dashboard (paid plans)

**Skip if:** You need full episode editing (use Descript), you publish fewer than 2 videos per month, or you need an offline tool.

## FAQ

**Q: What is Opus Clip?**
A: Opus Clip is an AI-powered video repurposing tool that converts long-form videos into short clips for TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts. It uses AI to identify the most engaging moments, add captions, and reframe video for vertical formats automatically.

**Q: How accurate are Opus Clip's captions?**
A: Opus Clip claims 95% caption accuracy and in testing that held — a 45-minute podcast needed only 3 caption corrections across 22 clips. Accuracy drops on heavy accents or fast crosstalk in multi-speaker recordings.

**Q: Is the free plan actually usable?**
A: Yes. 60 credits = 60 minutes of input video per month. You can process one full podcast episode, review all clips, and get a real sense of quality before paying anything. Exports include watermarks on the free plan.

**Q: How does the credit system work?**
A: 1 credit = 1 minute of input video. Processing a 30-minute interview costs 30 credits. Credits don't roll over; they reset monthly. The Starter plan at $19/mo gives 150 credits (2.5 hours of input video).

**Q: How does Opus Clip compare to Descript?**
A: Opus Clip is specialized for short-clip creation; Descript is a full podcast and video editor. Opus Clip wins on automation speed; Descript wins if you also need to edit the source recording, remove filler words, or overdub audio.

**Q: Which platforms can Opus Clip publish to?**
A: TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, LinkedIn, Facebook, and X (Twitter). Direct scheduling is available on paid plans. Free plan requires manual download and upload.

**Q: What's a realistic keeper rate from Opus Clip?**
A: Solo podcast content: ~85% of clips are usable. Two-person interviews: ~60%. Webinar/presentation content: ~80%. The virality score is the best filter — clips scoring 75+ are almost always worth keeping.

**Q: Can teams use Opus Clip?**
A: Yes. The Pro plan ($49/mo or $29/mo annual) includes 2 seats. Business ($99/mo) covers 5 seats. All plans share a credit pool, so one team member's processing counts against the shared monthly limit.
