**Q: What is a Flesch Readability Score?**

The Flesch Reading Ease score rates text on a scale of 0\u2013100. Scores of 60\u201370 are considered standard (8th-9th grade). Higher scores mean easier reading. Most web content should target 60\u201380. Academic and legal writing typically scores below 30.

The average adult reads at 238 words per minute. 1,000 words takes approximately 4 minutes to read. Speaking the same content takes about 7-8 minutes at an average speaking pace of 130 words per minute.
