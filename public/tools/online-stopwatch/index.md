## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How accurate is this online stopwatch?**

Accurate to the centisecond using the browser's high-resolution Performance API \u2014 sufficient for workouts, cooking, meetings and presentations.

---

**Q: Does the stopwatch keep running if I switch tabs?**

Yes. Elapsed time is calculated from timestamps rather than tick counts, so switching tabs or minimising the browser does not affect accuracy.

---

**Q: How do I record lap times?**

Press the left pusher on the watch, the Lap button, or press L. Fastest lap is highlighted green, slowest in red.

---

**Q: Can I set a countdown timer for any duration?**

Yes. Switch to Countdown mode, enter any combination of hours, minutes and seconds up to 23:59:59, or pick a quick preset.

---

**Q: What are the keyboard shortcuts?**

Space starts and pauses. R resets. L records a lap in stopwatch mode. M switches between stopwatch and countdown.
