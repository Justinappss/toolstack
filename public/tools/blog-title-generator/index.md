**Q: How many characters should a blog title be?**

For SEO, keep blog titles under 60–70 characters so Google doesn't truncate them in search results. For social media and email, slightly longer titles can work well. All titles generated by this tool stay within the 70-character SEO limit by default.

**Q: What makes a good blog title?**

A great blog title does three things: it targets a specific keyword or search intent, it creates an emotional response (curiosity, urgency, desire), and it sets a clear expectation for what the reader will get. Specificity dramatically outperforms vague titles — '7 Proven Ways to Double Instagram Followers in 30 Days' outperforms 'How to Get More Instagram Followers' every time.
