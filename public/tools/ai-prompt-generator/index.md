## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What is a prompt framework?**

A prompt framework is a structured template that tells an AI model exactly what role to play, what to do, how to think, and what format to return. Frameworks like RISEN and Chain-of-Thought consistently produce higher quality outputs than plain requests because they eliminate ambiguity and give the model complete context.

---

**Q: What is RISEN prompting?**

RISEN stands for Role, Instructions, Steps, End Goal, and Narrowing. It is one of the most effective prompt engineering frameworks because it gives the AI complete context: who it is, what to do, how to do it, what the goal is, and what constraints to follow. RISEN prompts consistently outperform generic requests.
